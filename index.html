TAEKWONDO SPARRING GEARS

  • Taekwondo is a Korean martial art.
  • The origins of Taekwondo can be traced back over 2,000 years to ancient Korea.
  • The earliest records of Korean martial arts date back to the kingdom of Goguryeo in the first century BCE.
  • The Japanese occupation of Korea from 1910 to 1945 had a profound impact on the development of modern Taekwondo.
  • After Korea gained independence in 1945, efforts were made to revive and unify the martial arts of Korea.
  • Korean martial arts leaders came together and decided to unify the different styles under the name "Taekwondo." The name "Taekwondo" was officially adopted on April 11, 1955.
  • In recent years, there has been an effort to unify various Taekwondo styles and organizations under the World Taekwondo (WT).
  • Today, Taekwondo remains an integral part of Korean heritage and continues to inspire individuals to cultivate physical fitness, mental strength, and personal growth.
